# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 8, 2026**

## Introduction

Jaboi ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo URL

### 2. Location Data
We collect your approximate location (city-level) using your IP address to:
- Suggest destinations in your region
- Calculate approximate travel distances
- Estimate trip costs

We do NOT collect your precise GPS location.
Your city-level location is never shared with third parties.

### 3. Travel Preferences
We store your:
- Visited destinations
- Saved destinations
- Trip plans and itineraries
- Budget preferences
- Travel interests (e.g., Nature, Culture, Food)

### 4. Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- App interaction data (pages viewed, features used)
- Crash reports (if enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- **Personalize recommendations**: AI-powered suggestions based on your preferences
- **Save your trips**: Store itineraries and travel plans
- **Calculate distances**: Estimate travel times and costs
- **Improve the app**: Analytics and crash reporting
- **Send notifications**: Trip reminders and travel tips (with your permission)

## Data Storage

- **Cloud Storage**: Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore)
- **Local Storage**: Preferences and settings stored on your device
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted using HTTPS encryption

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing outside the app

We **MAY** share data with:
- **Google Firebase**: For secure cloud storage and authentication
- **Gemini AI**: For generating personalized itineraries (anonymized)
- **Google Maps**: For location services and place information

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Export all your data anytime
- **Delete**: Permanently delete your account and all data
- **Update**: Modify your preferences and settings
- **Opt-out**: Disable location, notifications, or analytics

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts**: Data retained as long as account exists
- **Deleted accounts**: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Cached data**: May remain on device until cleared

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last updated" date.
